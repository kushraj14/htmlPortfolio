Mammals

Mammals are a diverse and widespread group of warm-blooded vertebrates characterized by several key features. These features set them apart within the animal kingdom. Here is a brief description of mammals:

Mammals comprise a vast array of species, including humans, elephants, whales, bats, and rodents. This group's adaptability, diverse ecological roles, and complex social behaviors contribute to their success in nearly every corner of the globe.
